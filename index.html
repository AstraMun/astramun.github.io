Committees & Agendas

Flagship

💸 ECOFIN

United Nations Economic & Financial Committee

Assessing measures for economic reconstruction of post conflict regions with special emphasis on Ukraine.

🚀 COPUOS

UN Committee On the Peaceful Uses of Outer Space

Addressing the militarization of outer space and develop and strengthen the legal framework governing the same.

❄️ UNSC

United Nations Security Council

Deliberating upon the possible implications of Arctic Militarisation on the peace and security in the region.

🇮🇳 Lok Sabha

Indian Parliament

Indo-Pak conflict: Reflect on military action, defense, tech preparedness with urgent emphasis on fortifying the North East frontier.

Flagship

🌐 WTO

World Trade Organisation

Deliberation upon the implementation of multilateral trade policies to counter climate change effects on global trade and commerce with special focus on fragile economies.

🏛 UNHRC

United Nations Human Rights Council

Deliberation on the escalating global manifestations of intolerance and prejudice with emphasis on the socio-political instability arising from unfounded narrations, cultural misconceptions, and regional biases.

📸 IP

International Press

Journalists & Photographers - Document the conference, conduct interviews, and create compelling stories about the proceedings.
